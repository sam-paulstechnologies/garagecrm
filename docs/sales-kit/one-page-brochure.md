# SayaraForce One-Page Brochure Copy

## Headline

Recover missed leads. Retain more garage customers.

## What It Is

SayaraForce is a WhatsApp-first lead and retention CRM built for UAE garages.

It helps garages capture enquiries, assign follow-ups, track customer conversations, convert leads into bookings, manage jobs and invoices, and bring old customers back for repeat service.

## The Problem

Most garages lose money because follow-up is scattered:

- WhatsApp leads sit on personal phones.
- Missed calls and DMs are not tracked.
- Staff forget follow-ups after sending prices.
- Bookings, jobs, and invoices are disconnected.
- Old customers are rarely reminded to return.

## The SayaraForce Flow

Customer enquiry -> Lead -> Conversation -> Opportunity -> Booking -> Job -> Invoice -> Retention reminder

## Why Garages Use It

- See all active leads in one place.
- Track WhatsApp conversations and follow-up status.
- Know who owns each customer.
- Convert qualified leads into bookings.
- Track job and invoice progress.
- Identify old customers who should come back.
