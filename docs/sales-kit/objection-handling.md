# SayaraForce Objection Handling

## "We already use WhatsApp."

Exactly. SayaraForce does not replace WhatsApp as the customer channel. It adds tracking, ownership, follow-up, bookings, jobs, invoices, and retention around WhatsApp.

## "My staff will not use a complicated CRM."

The first workflow is simple: see lead, reply/follow up, qualify, book, job, invoice. We avoid showing advanced settings in the first rollout.

## "Is this expensive?"

The goal is to recover missed work. If the garage recovers a few jobs per month, the system can pay for itself.

## "Can we keep our WhatsApp Business app?"

Possibly, depending on Meta/WABA setup and coexistence requirements.

FOUNDER ACTION REQUIRED: decide coexistence policy before promising it.

## "Do you send campaigns?"

SayaraForce is campaign-ready, but the first rollout focuses on lead recovery and retention safety. Campaigns should start only after WhatsApp templates and consent are approved.

## "What about customer privacy?"

Customer data should be imported and messaged only with lawful basis and garage approval. Final legal terms should be approved before paid rollout.
