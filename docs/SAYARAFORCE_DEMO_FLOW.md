# SayaraForce First Demo Flow

Goal: show the operational journey, not every module.

## Safe 15-Minute Flow

1. Admin login.
2. Admin dashboard.
3. Manager login.
4. Manager dashboard.
5. Manager inbox.
6. Create or capture a lead.
7. Convert lead to client/opportunity.
8. Move opportunity to booking.
9. Create job.
10. Create invoice.
11. Show retention/dashboard angle.
12. Open on mobile.

## Demo Narrative

A customer messages or submits a lead. SayaraForce captures it, assigns it, tracks the conversation, converts it to a booking, creates the job, raises an invoice, and later reminds the garage to bring the customer back.

## Do Not Show In First Demo

- Full settings.
- Raw webhook screens.
- Meta/WABA technical setup.
- Every report.
- Every role.
- Payment setup.
- Campaign tracking.
- Advanced retention configuration.
- Anything that needs an apology or long explanation.

## Blocker Rule

If a screen feels embarrassing or needs a long explanation, mark it as a blocker and skip it in the first demo.
