# SayaraForce 15-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-1:30 - Positioning

"SayaraForce helps UAE garages recover missed leads and retain more customers through a WhatsApp-first CRM."

Show the simple journey:

Lead -> Opportunity -> Booking -> Job -> Invoice -> Retention

## 1:30-3:00 - Admin Dashboard

Show the owner view:

- Leads.
- Bookings.
- Jobs.
- Invoices.
- Retention angle.

## 3:00-5:00 - Manager Dashboard and Inbox

Show how a manager sees conversations and daily operational work.

Do not send a real WhatsApp message unless founder-approved test mode is active.

## 5:00-7:00 - Lead Capture

Create or open a lead.

Show:

- Phone/contact context.
- WhatsApp-first follow-up.
- Lead status.

## 7:00-9:00 - Qualify Lead to Opportunity

Move the lead to Qualified.

Explain that the opportunity is created or reused safely.

## 9:00-11:00 - Booking

Move the opportunity to Booking Confirmed.

Show booking date, slot, and manager confirmation flow.

## 11:00-13:00 - Job and Invoice

Convert booking to job.

Complete the job with invoice data.

Show the invoice status.

## 13:00-15:00 - Retention

Explain how completed service history becomes future reminders and repeat business.

Close with:

"The goal is not more software. The goal is fewer missed leads and more returning customers."
